Instruments still in their coffins

Despite it's reputation there are amazingly some redeeming qualities about the banjo, what differs the banjo from your average guitar is that whilst the first 4 strings will be tuned to notes in a similar fashion, the highest pitch note will actually be at the top above the lowest pitch one. Which allows for the banjo to produce that famous twang. I didn't get far.

The Saxophone, backing track to love songs you cant recall the name of - and the migraines of my neighbours when I practised. The only brass member of the woodwind family, in the hands of an amateur it just manages to pass for torture.

Like many teenage boys throughout history, I figured my ticket to the opposite gender would be rock stardom via the electic guitar.

Like many teenage boys throughout history, I didn't pull it off.
